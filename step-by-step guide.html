Step-by-Step Guide to Become a Social Media Manager

  1. Obtain a degree in Marketing, Communication, or Multimedia.
  2. Gain experience by managing social media accounts (freelance/intern).
  3. Learn analytics tools (e.g., Meta Business Suite, Google Analytics).
  4. Build a strong personal online brand and portfolio.
  5. Stay updated with social media trends & platform updates.
  6. Apply to companies like Samsung or creative agencies.
